Before filling in the form
it is vital that you read and understand the information posted in the performers page, particularly under the heading Outside Locations and/or The Tent on the Square, whichever is appropriate. This is important, even if you've made applications in the past - there are changes this year.

If you're applying for The Tent on the Square, write "Tent" in the Location box on page 2 of the form.
