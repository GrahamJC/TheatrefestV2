our partners and supporters in 2017

Barnstaple Town Centre Management are our partners in this enterprise.



Barnstaple Town Council have made availabe St Anne's Arts Centre and The Guildhall.



Celebrate England Big Lottery Fund supports the outside performances.



Samuels Solicitors are sponsoring the programme in The Guildhall



The Bridge Trust have given us a grant towards the costs of the Tent on the Square.



Rotary Club of Barnstaple has awarded a grant towards costs associated with the Tent.



The Golden Lion Tap is providing us with a venue and a box-office hub.



Park School are loaning us their staging system for the Baptist Hall seating.



Smiths Solicitors have made a donation to help with venue costs.



Barnstaple Coffee Shop, as in previous years, are hosting afternoon 'taster' sessions.



Rosie Bracher Solicitors have supported the advertising.



bikeshed are hosting a number of informal performances.



white moose are hosting a performance in the gallery.



Barnstaple Museum is hosting performances and helping with facilities.



North Devon Councillors are suporting Fringe TheatreFest with community grants.



NDADA have given us space in one of their re:store shops to sell tickets



St John's Garden Centre make available a truck to transport bulky equipment.



Fullabrook CIC have made it possible to fully equip the tent as a venue.
