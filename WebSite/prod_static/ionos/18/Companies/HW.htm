Helen Wood

Join Ordnance Survey enthusiast, Helen Wood, for a humorous exploration of the much-loved, fold-out, paper map. Take a fascinating, laughter-filled, virtual ramble with sheet no. 168, Explorer map, and celebrate the simple beauty of Britain’s inspiring national treasure. O.S. fanatics, O.S. curious and O.S sceptics are all welcome at the O.S. Map Fan Club.

Awarded the Edinburgh Festival Fringe sell-out show laurel in 2017.

Awarded Best Original Act at Swindon Fringe Festival.
