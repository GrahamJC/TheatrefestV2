Five minute 'tasters' from up to a dozen companies to help you decide what you must see. Get ahead of the game by spotting the sell-out shows before the tickets have all gone.

Also an opportunity to meet performers, some of the great volunteer team that put Fringe TheatreFest together and other audience members.
