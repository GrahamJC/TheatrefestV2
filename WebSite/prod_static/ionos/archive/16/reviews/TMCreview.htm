You Have Mail

The Monday Collective


There are not many theatre previews that announce that the show you are going to see is just ‘a game’, but that is precisely what this performance piece by The Monday Collective is; an interactive game.

Interactive and participatory theatre is not usually my thing but by the end of the 45 minute run time, I had thoroughly enjoyed myself (I can’t say why…I’m sworn to secrecy)

The maximum of eight people per performance ensures that everybody who sees this show can participate to the full, and believe me, you will want to join in.

You Have Mail will appeal to all age groups, families especially should give it a go, and of course, all the grown-ups who are still kids at heart.

Weird, wacky and totally fun, you have to try this!
Sally Wells, The Daily Fringe
