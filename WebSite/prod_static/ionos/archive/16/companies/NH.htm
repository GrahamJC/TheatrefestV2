Szgrabble!

Nick Hall

comedy-drama
age 12+
60 mins

  • Room At The Top
  • Saturday 6.15pm & 10.15pm
  • Sunday 2pm

Comedian Nick Hall brings his one-man Cold War thriller to the Barnstaple Fringe. In this fast-paced, energetic and hilarious multi-character show, Nick brings to life a vivid world of spying, suspense, seduction and most importantly - Scrabble!


Nick has been performing solo comedy since 2013. His fast-paced, energetic, and hilarious style has already marked him out as one to watch. He's reached the semi-finals of So You Think You’re Funny, beaten the Gong at The Comedy Store, beaten the Blackout at Up the Creek, and won the New Act Night at the Comedy Café.

Nick has appeared on BBC2, BBC 3 & BBC Radio 4, as well as writing for BBC Radio 4 & ITV2.

Nick took his debut solo character comedy show to the Underbelly for the 2015 Edinburgh Fringe. Nick also performed in the Adam Riches’ show ‘Coach Coach’ at the 2015 Edinburgh Fringe and Soho Theatre.
