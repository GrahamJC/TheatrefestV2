Mind Over Matt

Matt Cann

theatre
age 15+
50 mins

  • gallery@QT
  • Thursday 9.45pm
  • Saturday 4pm
  • Sunday 5.30pm

Mind over Matt is a solo performance which aims to demonstrate what it's like to live with ADHD and looks at issues such as sexuality and where babies come from.

It's also a light hearted and honest approach to existence and all the things that can ever go wrong. Live music, Dance, Spoken word and Comedy are all used to tell the story with a touch of improvisation, and complete randomness. With some adult humour.

That’s if it doesn’t all go wrong (again).

Perhaps he should have taken those psychostimulants after all. Mind over Matt is now developing into another piece of theatre which begins to look a bit more directly at ADHD/ADD.

Matt is a Performer & Technician from North Devon who has recently finished his BA degree in Theatre & Performance arts at Plymouth University.
