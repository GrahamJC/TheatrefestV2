9ft²

A Hammier Basis

comedy
age 12+ strong language
45 mins

  • St Anne's Arts Centre
  • Thursday 8.30pm
  • Friday 10pm
  • Saturday 1pm

Space is a premium commodity now. So many people are fitting into small spaces. We fit closer together and tessellate as much as we can, but we're still lonely. And lonely people do silly things like a contortionist getting trapped in their own box, a captain that refuses to go down with the ship or two friends buried alive together playing “I spy”.

A Hammier Basis' production is a series of monologues and duologues all taking place in an area 3 feet by 3 feet, looking at the people we share space with, scared, lonely, silly people who just want to fit in.

Find out more about the production and the company at www.ahammierbasis.weebly.com
