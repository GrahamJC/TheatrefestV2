Bondagers

Yo Yo

dance theatre
age 12+
30 mins

  • studio@QT
  • Saturday 2.15pm

Based on a play of the same name, Bondagers draws on Sue Glover's lyrical writing, depicting the lives of female agricultural labourers on a Scottish Borders farm in 1860.

Dancers embody the rhythm of the land, navigating toil, hope and humour in beautifully crafted dance theatre.
