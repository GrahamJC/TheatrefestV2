Granny Smith

Theatre Transformations

family, mask, interactive
ages 5+
45 mins

  • The Baptist Hall
  • Friday 5.15pm
  • Saturday 4pm
  • Sunday 5pm

Granny Smith loves cooking so much she used to teach it in Britain. But when she retired she indulged her other love – France. Now settled there, she chatters in English and French about her gymnastics classes, her grandchildren, her life in France – and of course, her cooking.

Join us for a show full of humour and gentle instruction on language, cooking and French culture. You might visit her home to browse her recipe books and try out your French. She’ll show you how to make a great mint tea – which you can share afterwards, with French pear and chocolate crumble and a demonstration of masks.


Theatre Transformations run by Tracey Boot, is a French –Scottish masked theatre company based in Chambery in Savoie, France.
