Snakes! The Musical

Quite Nice Theatre

musical comedy
age 14+ contains some strong language
70 mins

  • studio@QT
  • Friday 5.45pm
  • Saturday 4.15pm
  • Sunday 6.45pm

Prior to its West End run, the smash hit "cult classic" of the 2013 Edinburgh Fringe is coming to Barnstaple!

A young director has written a musical about some snakes on a plane. With the help of two actors he hopes to wow a group of West End producers to secure the £50million he needs to turn his show into the West End mega-musical he knows it can be. Join our hopeful dreamers as they guide you through what you can expect when it finally transfers to the Palladium!

Packed full of catchy, original musical numbers this award winning show looks at the darker side of the theatre industry and examines the compromise between artistic merit and box office returns.
