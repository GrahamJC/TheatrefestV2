Confessions of a Rabbi's Daughter

Emily Rose


musical-theatre
age 12+
60 mins

  • gallery@QT
  • Friday 5.30pm
  • Saturday 10.15pm
  • Sunday 5.15pm

Just as the Rabbi’s daughter becomes engaged to her perfect match, she realises her feelings for her best friend Sarah. Watch as Rachel faces the choice between her heart and her dreams through drama and song.

The show enjoyed a performance at the York New Musical Festival, a successful run in the Edinburgh Fringe last summer and an Off-Broadway run earlier this year.


Emily Rose is an English singer-songwriter, comedienne and musical-theatre writer. As well as gigging up and down the UK, she has an MA in composition from Bristol University, has been commissioned by the London Jewish Male Choir and will be attending NYU’s prestigious Graduate Musical Theatre Writing Program.
