When Dick Privet is hired to find Jonny Volent, he finds he's not alone. Femme fatale, Lola Meme is in deep. Together they witness a murder and try to out-fox the heavies. Who is the man calling himself The Collector? Will Dick get answers? Results? Corpses? A date with Lola?
