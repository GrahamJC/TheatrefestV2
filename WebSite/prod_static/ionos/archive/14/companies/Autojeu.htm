Purgatory Passengers

Autojeu Theatre

comedy-drama
ages 10+
50 mins

  • Room At The Top
  • Thursday 8pm
  • Friday 8pm
  • Saturday 3pm & 6pm
  • Sunday 2pm

Come join us at an airport. The check-in is closing, as the clock ticks away some rather unusual chaps appear out of the wood work. Expect fart noises, exploding chickens, and a departure like no other!

“Sam Gibbs is undoubtedly talented and versatile and his noises are diverse and entertaining, as are the expressions on his elastic face.”
