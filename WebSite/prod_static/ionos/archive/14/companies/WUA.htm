Women of an Uncertain Age

Flip Webster and Maggie Bourgein

comedy sketch show
14+ mild naughtiness!
60 mins

  • gallery@QT
  • Thursday 8pm
  • Saturday 4.15pm
  • Sunday 1pm

Fifty Shades of Grey Hair – Girls just wanna have fun even when they’re over 45 …ish!

Flip and Maggie are back! Older but no better behaved, they bring you new sketches, songs and characters plus some old favourites. Through character-led comedy, song and dance, Flip and Maggie share coffee ice cream, hip hop moves and their take on life in the 21st century. Who says ‘women of a certain age’ are too old for careers, sex and a banging social life?

Featuring Woman’s Hour’s Dame Jenni Murray

 

Flip’s work includes The Musketeers, Silk, Wallander and New Tricks, Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire and Cabin Pressure.

Maggie started her career with the D’Oyly Carte Opera Company and has appeared in the West End and on tour in comedies and musicals and with Topping and Butch.

 

Sell out performances Fringe Theatrefest and Ignite 2012 and Edinburgh 2013

‘Dynamic, entertaining and borderline inappropriate.’ Fringe Secrets

‘Shining moments of brilliance’ Threeweeks Edinburgh

‘The show hits some refreshingly different targets and, at last, provides something (many things) for the over-50s to empathize with!’ Alistair McGowan

Suitable for both sexes and anyone over 14.
