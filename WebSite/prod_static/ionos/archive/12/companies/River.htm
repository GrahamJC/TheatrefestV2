Revlon Scarlet Rescues the Crown Jewels

River Productions & Skylight Theatre

Pirate Revlon Scarlet, time traveller, returns from Camden Fringe 2010. Teaming up with his sister Sabre, they battle evil Captain Bligh to rescue the crown jewels for Queen Elizabeth's 1952 coronation. Meeting various historical figures on their adventures, this educational yet entertaining show will be performed in a marquee on The Square.

Best suited to 6-11 year-olds and their families.
