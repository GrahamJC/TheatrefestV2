A Pirate's Life for Me

Vagabondi Puppets

Join old Captain Mary MacGuire and her trusty first mate Annie Greengrass as they tell you everything they know about a life on the high seas. They will tell you about their piratical past with the help of puppets, songs, maps and an incredible unfolding ship. But be warned landlubbers - don't believe a word!
