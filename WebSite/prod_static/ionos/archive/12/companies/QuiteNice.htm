This Was Your Life

Quite Nice Theatre

Should he go to heaven or should he go to hell? You decide.

This Was Your Life is a comedy play with music in which the audience are invited to the recording of everyone's favourite reality show to determine the eternal fate of a recently deceased contestant.

The play promises to be funny, moving and - with two possible endings - unpredictable!

A cast of 3 actors take on over 20 roles and sing some of the catchiest original musical numbers around. With TWO possible endings this show is sure to be one you won’t forget.


Winner of 3 awards at the Stratford Fringe Festival:
BEST ACTOR, BEST DIRECTOR, BEST ORIGINAL SONG
