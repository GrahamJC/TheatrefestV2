Snarks

Entertainingly Different Youth Theatre

An inspired piece of nonsense by Philip Bailey based on Lewis Carroll's 'Hunting of the Snark' and other nonsense verse.

The company is made up of senior members of 'Entertainingly Different' drama group who are aged 14-19 years and rehearse weekly in North Devon.

The show is very physical and is suitable for the whole family with its colourful characters, adventure, slapstick and general silliness.
