Tramps Like Us

Autojeu Theatre

Two homeless men share their wisdom and worldly knowledge as they bicker about the immoral nature of St George, blisters gained on the pilgrimage to Mecca and the comfort and warmth that can be found in one simple piece of cardboard. With the world falling apart around them these two men start to rebuild it, one old rusty baked bean can at a time.

 

 

About Autojeu

Autojeu Theatre was founded in 2009 by Sam Gibbs after his studies in Paris under legendary teacher Philippe Gaulier. They dedicate themselves to producing playful and imaginative work.
