Tea Anyone?

Pidge Productions

"We're new here..Do you want to come in for a cup of tea?"

This year we have managed to move on and have found ourselves trying to make our own homes in new places. With our audience we will chat and tell stories over a cup of tea and possibly some nibbles.

Can we make a space into a home and strangers into a family in just 45 minutes?
