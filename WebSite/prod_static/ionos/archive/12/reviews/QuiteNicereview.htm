This Was Your Life

Quite Nice Theatre


A multi talented cast of three entertain us with comedy and songs; inviting the audience to participate in the biggest decision of Michael’s ‘afterlife’; does he deserve to spend his eternity in heaven or hell? The facts of his life are presented to us from former friends and family, including the ‘star’ of the show Mimi, the German cabaret singer. There is the opportunity to pose questions to the deceased before you vote on whether he ascends or descends; this is a chance to show your true colours! Quite Nice Theatre performs for a thoroughly enjoyable hour, do try to see this performance, you won’t be disappointed. A truly exceptional piece of Fringe Theatre.
