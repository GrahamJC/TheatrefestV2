A Pirate's Life for Me

Vagabondi Puppets


Now let’s be honest here, who doesn’t want to be a pirate? The lures of plundering for treasure, swash-buckling showdowns and cool hats are just too much to ignore. So, understandably, you want to become a pirate. But where are you going to learn the trade? Here! Two pirates take you through the basics of pirate lingo, climbing the rigging and scrubbing the decks. You’ll learn all the latest Pirate songs, and you can even help write the lyrics to one yourself; although it has to be said, when a group of young aspiring crew members are asked to come up with a word that rhymes with ‘two’, there’s only going to be one outcome. Brilliant family fun, and you might just learn that treasure doesn’t have to be gold or silver.
