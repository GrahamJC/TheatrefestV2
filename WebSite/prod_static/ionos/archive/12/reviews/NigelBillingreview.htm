The Blizzard

Nigel Billing


'The Blizzard' is a fascinating tale about three men and their life at sea as cargo carriers during the late 19th century. Combining music and story telling makes for an entertaining piece where the characters face many hardships as they make their way through forceful winds and rough seas to deliver their shipment. Nigel Billing gives a superb performance in this solo act as his vivid descriptions recreate the life of a Cornish sea worker for the audience to experience themselves. Based partially on truth, this story is sure to captivate you.
