Links With Lit

Peter Castle


Join folk singer and storyteller, Pete Castle at The Boston Tea Party Theatre for a memorable evening of short stories, poems and acoustic folk - songs. The show Links with Lit assembles stories from some of the greatest literary writers of all time, Thomas hardy, William Shakespeare, William Chaucer and many others. Pete Castle also brings his considerable skill as a folk singer to the mix. So if you are a great lover of literature, have an interest in short stories or simply want to experience something new and one of a kind – than Pete Castle’s Links with Lit is a performance you definitely won’t want to miss.
