Tea Anyone?

Pidge Productions


Tea Anyone? Is an upbeat, funny and uplifting play about two people who try to make The Boston Tea Party Theatre feel more like a place unlike any other: home. This show, brought to you by Pidge productions, will make you laugh, make you smile, make you want a cup of tea and yet all the while make you think about a handful of questions that you may not have considered before, about some of the most important topics in today’s society: what is it that makes a place feel more like a home? How do you get guests to leave a house warming party? And finally, the most important question of all: Tea Anyone?
