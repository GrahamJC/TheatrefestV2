Dr Frankenstein's Travelling Freak Show

Tin Shed Theatre Co


Tin Shed Theatre Co. brings you Mary Shelly’s story with a twist! The tale is portrayed through a bizarre and strange cast of circus freaks, the leader of which is an unhinged and twisted ringmaster, narrating the story and playing most of the female roles. His handful of clumsy, inept freaks are his supporting cast including the Bearded Lady, a mind-reading man (who is also part lobster), a monkey puppet and of course the star of the show, Frankenstein’s monster himself. Dr Frankenstein’s Travelling Freak Shows is a theatre performance unlike any other: hilarious, dramatic and often strange - this play is a must see that will keep the audience guessing.
