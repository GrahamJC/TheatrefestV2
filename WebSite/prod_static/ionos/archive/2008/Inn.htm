The Inn on the Square

The Inn on the Square is a handsome historical building in the centre of Barnstaple.

Recently under new management, The Inn has embraced the Fringe TheatreFest wholeheartedly. They have made available a large space that is well-known in the town as a rock music venue. With the help of North Devon Theatres this will be converted into a studio theatre for the duration of the Fringe TheatreFest.

The theatre-space has a separate entrance and there will be no alcohol in the studio itself so younger audience members will be permitted. Though generally speaking the programme at The Inn is for older audiences.

Of course all the facilities of The Inn will be available in between performances, including meals. The Inn are offering a 10% discount on food for "Frequent Fringer" ticket holders. The Inn also provides accommodation - check out the website - www.innonthesquare.co.uk
