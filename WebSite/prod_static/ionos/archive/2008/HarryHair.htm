Harry Hair Cabaret Show

A Modern Clown Comedy with few words

Harry Hair, entertainer extraordinaire, enjoys himself enormously performing in front of his audience. Unfortunately his shows don't always go quite as planned....

The comedy is subtle provoking double-chinned laughter.

Harry Hair Cabaret Show is being performed for the first time in England here at the North Devon Fringe Festival.

Written and performed by Anthony Trahair
Directed by Piero Partigianoni

Anthony Trahair was born in London but grew up in Cornwall. He has been in Europe since 1996 and owes his professional training to numerous years of street performing, combined with his recent studies at the Dimitri Theatre School in Switzerland - a school of movement theatre and theatrical creation from which he graduated, after three years of intense work, in 2006.
