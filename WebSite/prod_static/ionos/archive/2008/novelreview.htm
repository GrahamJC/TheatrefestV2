First Impressions - a novel experience

Five seats on stage, five actors on seats, fifteen novels from the programme’s list of thirty two, introduced by one or more actors reading from the opening pages for the audience to identify.  Well known past and current titles, very well presented with enough clues to jog memories for the chance to win a very nice bottle of wine. ‘Novel experience’ indeed.

Ginny Hobart (official Fringe reviewer)
