Jam the Gypsy

Jam is travelling the West Country and drops in to share a story with children.

If you are feeling creative you might have time to make a new one with him before he wanders on his way.

Enjoy a story and the chance to create a story with Peter Burford, a man of many parts. A former member of the legendary Medium Fair Theatre Company, Peter is also appearing in fringe08 theatrefest with the White Hippos in Purple is for Sarcasm - at The Inn on the Square - and has directed the company in The Sparkling Fragile at The Factory.
