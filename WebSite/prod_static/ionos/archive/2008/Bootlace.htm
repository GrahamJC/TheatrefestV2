Becoming Mum: A Cautionary Tale

Bootlace Productions present a mumologue!

You don't think it's going to happen to you, until one day you look in the mirror and find your mother staring back. And not only that - you have also become invisible.

Elizabeth is approaching one of those “big” birthdays and, she doesn’t want to but it’s making her question when and how she began turning into her mother.

Come and join her as she meanders through her past and contemplates her future.

Please feel free to wear purple!
