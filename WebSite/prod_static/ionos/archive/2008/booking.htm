tickets are now on sale

Advance tickets are available at The Queens Theatre, Barnstaple and The Landmark, Ilfracombe. Telephone: 01271 32 42 42

Tickets will also be available on the day at each venue one hour prior to the performance (subject to availability).


Prices: £4/£2 concessions. A Frequent Fringer ticket at £15 will allow admission to 5 performances - plus a 10% discount on food at The Inn on the Square.


Everyone aged over 13 will need to be wearing a Fringe Button to enter the theatre. These can be purchased from the venues for £1. Money from the Fringe Buttons helps to provide the infra-structure for the Fringe. Money from ticket sales goes directly to the performers.
