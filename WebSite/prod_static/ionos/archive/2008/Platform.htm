The Platform

The Platform is located within The Old Railway Station on The Strand a few yards from The Civic Centre. Owned by North Devon District Council, the building presently houses Pathfield School's 6th Form.

The Platform is an historic building of great charm and character and is widely used for meetings and seminars. With the help of North Devon Theatres the space will become a fully operational studio theatre for the duration of the Fringe Theatrefest.
