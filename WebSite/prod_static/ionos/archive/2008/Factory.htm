The Factory

The Factory was the only Fringe TheatreFest venue last year and was then just starting to establish itself. It is now very much part of the North Devon scene - check out the website - www.thefactoryonline.co.uk

The Factory boasts a large, versatile main space that accomodates North Devon College's music performance courses as well as hosting a whole range of public performance activity. Also a smaller foyer area that will be used for inter-performance musical entertainment during the Fringe TheatreFest.

A large stage and plenty of head-room means that this will be the venue for the dance element of the TheatreFest as well as offering a full range of other theatre fare.
