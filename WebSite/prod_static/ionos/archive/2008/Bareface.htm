Knights of the Lonely Hearts

Barefaced Cheek are a South West theatre company writing and performing original comedy. Their sketch show Knights of the Lonely Hearts was first performed at Theatre Upstairs at the Globe (Exeter) in October 07. Barefaced Cheek are planning to take their show on the road all the way to Edinburgh for the Fringe Festival in 2009.

Barefaced Cheek are Derek Keeping, Lianne Perry and Sean Devlin. Knights of the Lonely Hearts is directed by Kathryn Beaty. For news and updates on future performances by Barefaced Cheek, visit www.barefacedcheek.bravehost.com or email barefacedcheek@hotmail.co.uk.
