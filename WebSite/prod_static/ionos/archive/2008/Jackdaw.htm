Arrant Knaves All

Jackdaw Theatre present a celebration of some of theatre's most wicked, knavish and dastardly Men and Women.

Orlando, a well seasoned actor of indeterminate age, recalls and recounts some of the highlights and lowpoints of his illustrious and long-suffering career, bringing to life characters from plays from Shakespeare & Co to the present day - some well known, some not so well known.
