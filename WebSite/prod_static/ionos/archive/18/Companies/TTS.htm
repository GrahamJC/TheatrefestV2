‘Regal Tudor Gent seeks six wives to share a love of frequent banqueting. Must be able to tolerate tyrannical behaviour. Time wasters will be divorced or beheaded.’

WHO will Henry choose?

Come and join Total Theatre School in this humorous take on the Tudor King’s love life. Part historical fact, part game show, TTS journeys through his marital misadventures with plenty of laughs, music and dance.

HEADS WILL ROLL IN THIS HILARIOUS PARODY!


Now in its third year, Appledore-based Total Theatre School is an independently run, Saturday morning school open to children aged 4 – 16. Our aim is to boost confidence and help develop life skills through Drama, Singing & Dance. Whether looking for a career in the Performing Arts or simply wanting to have a go, we believe that every child can develop a sound sense of who they are and who they want to be by exploring in a safe, unpressurised and, above all, fun environment. We also offer support, guidance and training if a young person wishes to enter a LAMDA examination.
