Traditional Tales with a Twist 2

Entertainingly Different


Entertainingly Different are back at Fringe Theatrefest with a hugely entertaining take on two more well-known stories, Jack and the Beanstalk and Hansel and Gretel.

Fun, fast and colourful, this performance incorporates interactive story-telling with music and song and keeps the young audience captivated and totally involved throughout.

Visually there is plenty to catch the eye. The stage is well set out and the colourful props including wigs, masks and hats in bright primary colours become a rainbow blur of colour as Rob and Dex dash around the stage with boundless energy.

But the show isn’t just for the kids. The obvious rapport and the banter between the two performers, combined with their unbridled enthusiasm, show the talent and skill that Rob and Dex possess. A unique ability that can keep both children and adults entertained simultaneously.

This is a thoroughly entertaining, fun- filled hour that I would definitely recommend.
The Daily Fringe
