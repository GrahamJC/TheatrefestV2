IKEA the Whale

CAKE Productions


Ikea the Whale combines storytelling and mime with a message in an energetic performance by Cake Productions.

The combination of mime and storytelling directs the audience through the narrative at speed. Visually there is a lot to see on stage; props and characters are in bright, bold colours. This brightness combined with the constant and fast paced physical performance of Johan keeps eyes glued to the stage as he reacts to the story unfolding around him, often to comic effect.

Kids will love the colours, the songs and the story and the grown-ups in the audience won’t feel left out either as the narrative aims is aimed at them too. Shopping will never be the same again after this fun filled story has been seen.
Sally Wells, The Daily Fringe
