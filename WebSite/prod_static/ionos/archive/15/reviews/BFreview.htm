All Fall Apart

Blasted Fiction


Modern,smart, sassy and laugh out loud funny is the only way to describe this show by Blasted Fiction. As they open themselves up to scrutiny for the benefit of the paying public, the laughs come thick and fast and you can’t take your eyes off the stage.

The staging is simple, the lighting moody and the few props are minimal but it is all extremely effective and adds to the feel and flavour of the production.

The performances are solid and sublime all round and the fifty five minute run time goes far too quickly. I wish it were longer.

This is a superb piece of contemporary theatre and a definite must see before the weekend is over.
The Daily Fringe
