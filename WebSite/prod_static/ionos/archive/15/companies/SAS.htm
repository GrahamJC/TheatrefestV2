She Ain't Heavy, She's My Mother

Spit and Sawdust Theatre Company

drama, solo performance, verbatim
age 16+ strong language
55 mins

  • St Anne's Arts Centre
  • Friday 5.45pm
  • Saturday 8.45pm
  • Sunday 2.30pm

A one-man-show about a man growing up with a mother suffering from Anorexia.

This new, hard-hitting piece tells his story, as he unpacks the memories and experiences that shaped his childhood.

"Real experience laid bare ... revealing and uncompromising" (Exeunt Magazine).
