Sam and Lucy

dance theatre

"Two young ladies with bags of energy and talent" - Glyn Seal. Sam & Lucy come together for a second year to create Generation We. Inspired by human relations and fueled by their own experiences, the dancers explore the desire for connection through dynamic storytelling.
