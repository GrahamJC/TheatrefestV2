Aesop's Fables

Autojeu Theatre

comedy-theatre, family show
ages 5+
45 mins

  • The Baptist Hall
  • Thursday 6.30pm
  • Friday 9pm
  • Saturday 3pm

The Hare and the Tortoise set out to race, the Sun and the Wind keep a watchful eye and we have a Bear, a pessimistic tree and some other friends thrown in for good measure, this is Aesops Fables at their most ridiculous. A comedy for all ages to enjoy.
