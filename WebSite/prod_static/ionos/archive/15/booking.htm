booking information

tickets on sale from 2nd June


Full price: £5
Concessions: £3 (16 or under, students, over 60, unemployed, disabled)
Frequent Fringer Voucher: 6 shows for £15

You can buy your individual tickets and Frequent Fringer vouchers in advance from 2nd June through the Box Office, either in person or on the phone, or at the specified venues on the day of the show from one hour before the show starts. Frequent Fringer vouchers are also available on-line (please note a transaction fee of £2.60 is applicable). Make sure you keep yours in a safe place though, as lost Frequent Fringers cannot be replaced.


Fringe Buttons
Every audience member aged 13+ needs a Fringe Button to enter a venue.
These are available from any of the venues for £1 (only one purchase necessary). The money raised through the sale of Fringe Buttons provides vital funds towards the infrastructure costs of Fringe TheatreFest, whilst 100% of the ticket sales goes directly to the performers. Thank you for your support
