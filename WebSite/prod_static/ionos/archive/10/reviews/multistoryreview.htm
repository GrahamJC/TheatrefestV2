Medea

A gripping drama about betrayal, comes alive as Multi Story Theatre have the audience on the edge of their seats! Twisted logic leads to dreams of revenge, as the onlookers sympathize with Medea (Gill Nathanson), whilst feeling helpless to the outcome...

Playing a multitude of characters, Bill Buffery (Jason) and Gill create a moving and empowering performance. Having performed at the Royal Shakespeare Company Visitors Season, they understandably have followers, describing the drama as 'Amazing and Powerful' after the Thursday evening show. Guaranteed to leave you wanting more!

Annabelle Mantziou The Fringe Secrets
