Outspoken

A brilliant collection of the observations in life that leave you smiling from the start. A mixture of performance poetry, song, limericks, rude rhyme and found poems will have you in stitches! Rob Barratt and Jackie Juno present their work from the heart in a surprising - and sometimes raucous - manner! Everyday life is put to the test, and an intimate setting allows the audience to delve deep into the minds of these two fascinating people. Guaranteed giggles, and sure-fire anarchy, lead the way to a radiant show.

Annabelle Mantziou The Fringe Secrets
