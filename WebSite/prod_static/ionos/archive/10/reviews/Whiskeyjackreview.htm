A Pair of Shorts

The first of these two short comedy pieces is a funny 'first meeting' of boyfriend's mother! Called 'A Round With Alice', the classic 'cutting of the apron strings' is yet to happen, and the audience will be laughing and cringing as we watch three adorable characters move from love to hate in a lively and surprising tale of every girlfriend's worst fears.

The second treat from Whiskeyjack Productions sees a frustrated Dentist pursuing his acting career. Meanwhile his 'loyal' assistants are in the pub planning their dentistry revenge on unsuspecting clients! 'Dubious Practice' will have you following your dreams, and cancelling your dental appointment!!

Annabelle Mantziou The Fringe Secrets
