Funny Old Game: An A-Z of Football

A fantastically funny Grahame Lloyd presents an accessible comedy collection of all things Football. Whether you love the Game or not, you will find you self laughing at the articulate and smooth humorous songs and parodies. With a clever use of media and props, the stage comes alive with the sounds of the terraces, and some audience participation has been greatfully received, with the threat of a red card! The Talksport Commentator come self-confessed Football fanatic will have you laughing at the celebrity of Football, and the mistakes of the game too. A witty man, a quality show, and the promise of enjoyment. They think it's all over!...

Annabelle Mantziou The Fringe Secrets
