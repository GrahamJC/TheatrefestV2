Medea

multi story

An award winning pocket-sized update of Euripides' classic tale of love, hate and bloody revenge.

"A perceptive and alarmingly modern insight into the devastating power of perverted love and twisted logic.  A fabulous story, and powerful theatre. "

This production aroused tremendous excitement on a tour of theatres, arts centres and colleges in England - including performances in Stratford-upon-Avon as part of the RSC Visitors Season.

It took Canada by storm, chosen as best production at each of the three festivals it played.

At the Orlando International Fringe Theatre Festival, Medea won awards for Best Production and Best Female Performer.

"Miss it at your peril".
