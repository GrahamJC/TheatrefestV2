Floor Plan/(Untitled)

Openbatch Theatre

Openbatch bring two pieces to Fringe TheatreFest: one performance of Floor Plan, which played two performances last year, and two performances of (Untitled).

Floor Plan is a contemporary exploration of the enforced loss of individuality within a group set up. (Untitled) is their new piece devised through their current exploration of self.

Openbatch Theatre's first performance, Floor Plan has been received with great success and critical acclaim at, among others, the Dartington Campus Festival 2009, Theatrefest 2009, Dartington Residency 2009 and most recently it had its European Premier at the NEU/NOW festival in Vilnius in Lithuania, opening the festival in the city that is the new European capital of culture.
