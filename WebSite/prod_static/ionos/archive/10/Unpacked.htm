The Show Must Go On

Unpacked

An eccentric singing puppet arrives with her crew and their travelling piano (the guts of an old upright), and tell some of their own stories as they attempt to get the show under way.

An immersive, entertaining and highly interactive performance, using puppetry, object work, and live original music and singing.

This is a show that can happen anywhere, it's different every time. Come and join in, tell us your stories or sing a long to some favourite tunes.
