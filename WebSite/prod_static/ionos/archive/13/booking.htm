booking information

tickets on sale from the beginning of June


For each event:
Full price, £5
Concessions, £3 (16 or under, students, over 60, unemployed, disabled)
Available in advance from the Queen’s Theatre, Barnstaple and the Landmark Theatre, Ilfracombe. Please note that it is not possible to book Fringe TheatreFest tickets on-line - so please phone or go in person to either of the box offices. Tickets can also be purchased on the day of performance, at the appropriate venue, one hour before the performance starts.


Frequent Fringer Voucher, £15
Allows entry to 6 performances of your choice (provided seats are available).
These can be purchased in advance at the Queen’s and Landmark Theatres or from any of the venues during the Fringe TheatreFest weekend.


Fringe Buttons
Every audience member aged 13+ needs a Fringe Button to enter a venue.
These are available from any of the venues for £1 (only one purchase necessary). All income generated from ticket sales goes directly to the performers, but the money raised through the sale of Fringe Buttons provides vital funds towards the infrastructure costs of Fringe TheatreFest. Thank you for your support
