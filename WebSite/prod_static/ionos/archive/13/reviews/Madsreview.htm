...And...Here...And...There...And...

Mads Floor Andersen


...And...Here...And...There...And... is an abstract production by Mads Floor Andersen. It is focused on showing us a new perspective on the beautiful surroundings we take for granted on a daily basis. Throughout the day you can move freely through the area and watch the dynamic change of the seating as the performers and the public have free reign over how the environment develops and changes. The entire event is very organic with the seats being changed regularly into shapes, patterns and positioning. It is a very relaxing experience and fit for the whole family to spend time during the day between watching the other productions and exploring Barnstaple itself. However the piece is all based outside, so the mood can be dampened if it is raining. It is worth speaking to the makers of this production and having a very interesting discussion with him to see the thought processes behind this entire idea.
