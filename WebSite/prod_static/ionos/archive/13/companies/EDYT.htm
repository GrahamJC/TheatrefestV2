'All For the Want of a Charter' - Barum's Secrets and Lies

Entertainingly Different Youth Theatre

Connecting you with Barnstaple's past, uncovering some of the secrets she holds. Adapted from a play by Penny Griffiths this is a powerful piece of storytelling that will intrigue, delight and answer questions like Who were the four Mary's & Who was Barnstaple's most famous Son?
