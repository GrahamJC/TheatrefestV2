Death of a TV Salesman

L Vital

Vincey Boy sells fake jewellery on TV and lives a life of sordid excesses, manipulation, and falseness...until he has a meeting with death! A fast moving darkly comic tale that explores the darkest recesses of the human psyche. Will Vince survive the show?
