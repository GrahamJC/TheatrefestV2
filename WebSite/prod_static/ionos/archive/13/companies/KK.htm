If You Ever Leave Me

Kelly Kanayama

If You Ever Leave Me re-interprets Western folktales from an Asian-Pacific perspective to explore the family ties that bind us, the sacrifices we make in pursuit of a happy ending, and the stories we carry with us - whether or not we mean to.
