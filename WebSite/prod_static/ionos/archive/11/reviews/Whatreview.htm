Through the Eyes of a Journalist

What?Theatre Company


This is a tough and gritty performance tackling a tough and gritty subject. ‘Danny Vicks’ a successful international journalist finds himself back in the UK making a documentary on ‘Hidden Britain’. Danny is unsure of how much he can intervene to assist a young potential drug addict son of a stereotypical drunken father who survives on benefits and keeps order with the aid of his fists. Depicted through the mediums of film, mime and performance, Danny struggles with his own conscience; should he stay and help or simply walk away? A fine first appearance at the Barnstaple Fringe Theatrefest from this up and coming young theatre company.
