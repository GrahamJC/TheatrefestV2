For Jimmy

Lucy Lucy


Wow! Hold onto your seats for this intensely powerful and thought provoking seven minutes of theatre. Venue X really does provide the perfect setting for this most intimate rendition which is based on the true story of the untimel death of Angolan deportee Jimmy Mubenga.The story is depicted through the security guards restraining Mubenga on that fateful day, the last of his life. So real is this experience that as part of the audience, you could be on the plane, witnessing the events first hand. Although this is a short performance, the content is so intense, you leave believeing you have seen a full length production. The question is this, is this reminiscence a guilt trip or a true and factual explanation of events? That is for him to know and you as the audience to ponder on. What's certain is that this is an emotional rollercoaster. This is an extremely passionate performance and one that is highly recommended.
