Yo Yo


The introduction to this contemporary dance piece started with subtle lighting that was complemented by an apt choice of classical music and violin that set the scene. With only a ground sheet as a prop, the two dancers moved seamlessly from one set piece to the next, building atmosphere with their facial expressions and clever use of shadows. Beautiful poignant movements allowed both characters vulnerability to shine though as they danced with grace and elegance. The pain of the past and struggle to let go was highlighted with music and lighting as they entwined themselves in the sheet, adding layers of depth and meaning to this truly moving performance.
