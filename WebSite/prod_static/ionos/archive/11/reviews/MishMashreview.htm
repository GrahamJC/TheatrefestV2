A fantasic show with a very talented cast. The only negative comment I have is that the improvised singing section seemed very set up, it was a good idea but will only work if it is truly spur of the moment. The rest of the show was great, young lad was brilliant and the girl with the poem about her bedroom carpet was hillarious!

We are taken on a journey through a vibrant celebration and collection of poetry. This hyperactive ride is dripping with talent and brimful of creative ideas.

It has sheer energy and inventiveness that engages the audience with participation.

The stand out moment for me was the free verse of lyrics sang effortlessly to guitar music. It’s almost like taking a trip to ‘Alice in Wonderland.’ Simply wonderful.
