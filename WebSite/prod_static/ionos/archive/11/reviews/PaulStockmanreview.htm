Psychic or Trick of The Mind

Paul Stockman Mind Illusion Show


I did not believe Paul Stockman could infiltrate my brain, but he did. As I sat watching, I tried to second guess his next trick, yet found myself caught up in the moment, being swept along with the audience as he proved we are all influenced by the power of suggestion. He cleverly reads the audience, and involves everyone from the very beginning, with trickery and charm! Stockman makes accurate predictions, or is it just a lucky guess?! You definitely have to decide for yourself; is he Psychic?...or a trick of the mind?
