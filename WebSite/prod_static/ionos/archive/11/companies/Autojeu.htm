How to Climb Mount Everest

Autojeu Theatre

1 Planet, 1 Mountain, 1 Highest peak in the world, 1 Goal, 1 Man, 1 Shot, 1 Outcome? Come join us as we take part in one man's epic rise from sofa dwelling pizza eater to Mount Everest conqueror!

Mixing puppetry, live action, mime, and some audience interaction, Mount Everest is brought to your doorstep... and we'd like you to climb it! Autojeu invite the audience into their wacky world dripping in fun and play!

About Autojeu

Autojeu Theatre was founded in 2009 by Sam Gibbs after his studies in Paris under legendary teacher Philippe Gaulier. They dedicate themselves to producing playful and imaginative work. Currently based in Bristol, Autojeu's latest piece aims to focus on what the individual is capable of if he puts his heart to the task ahead.
