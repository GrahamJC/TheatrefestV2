tickets

on-line ticket sales are now open


Prices
Full price: £6
Concessions: £4 (16 or under, students, over 60, unemployed, disabled)

Frequent Fringer Voucher
You can buy a Frequent Fringer Voucher for £18 which can then be redeemed for one ticket to 6 different performances of your choice (working out at £3 per performance). Vouchers are not transferable.

Frequent Fringer Vouchers are not available online. They can only be bought in person from one of the Fringe TheatreFest box-offices (see below).

Online
Purchase full-price and concessions tickets on-line via this website. Either visit the webpage of the show you wish to book and click the 'Book Now' icon or click here for the full list.

There is a small handling-fee (51p per full-price ticket and 34p per concession) for on-line purchases which goes direct to TicketSource, the online ticketing company.

Frequent Fringer Vouchers cannot be purchased on-line.


Box-offices
From June 19, buy tickets (including Frequent Fringer Vouchers) with cash or by cheque from the Re:Store charity shop (on the corner of Boutport Street and Queens Street, Barnstaple) between 11am and 2pm, Mon - Sat.

During the festival, tickets and Frequent Fringer Vouchers will be on sale at The Baptist Hall, St Anne's Arts Centre and The Golden Lion Tap. Please keep your Frequent Fringers safe as they cannot be replaced if you lose them!


Pay-What-You-Will
Performances in The Guildhall and Room Upstairs are charging on a "Pay-What-You-Will" basis. No money changes hands until after the performance when you will be invited to contribute to a collection. We ask you to be guided by the price structure for regular venues - but not to feel embarrassed if you wish to contribute more!


Collecting Buckets
Some performances in informal settings - including out on the street - will be asking you to contribute to a "bucket collection". Please give generously as this is the only income these performers will have from the Festival. Perfomers are not paid a fee - only whatever their audience is willing to give.


Fringe Buttons
Every audience member aged 13+ needs a Fringe Button to enter a venue.
These are available from any of the venues for £1 (only one purchase necessary). The money raised through the sale of Fringe Buttons provides vital funds towards the infrastructure costs of Fringe TheatreFest, whilst 100% of the ticket price goes directly to the performers.

Thank you for your support.
