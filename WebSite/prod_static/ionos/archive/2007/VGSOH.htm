The Golden Key

multi story present their high-energy version of George Macdonald's well-loved tale.

Mossy and Tangle really don't like each other very much. But these two ordinary young people go on an extraordinary journey to find the Golden Key at the foot of the rainbow and to discover what lies hidden in the Chamber of the Most Precious.

Join them on their journey through the Valley of Shadows. Meet the Beautiful Grandmother and the Oldest Man in the World (a baby!). Can they escape a pair of ferocious French Maids and will they defeat the dreaded Shadow Master?

multi story have an international reputation for creating exciting and imaginative theatre for young people and their families.
