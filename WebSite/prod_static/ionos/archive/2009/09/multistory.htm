Sealskin

multi story


"I look in the mirror and it isn't me I see.
Only the sea. The sea.
This land on which I roam
It isn't home, it isn't home."

This is the song their mother sang but what did she mean?

Siblings sift through the memories of their childhood trying to untangle the truth about their mother and what happened to her when she mysteriously disappeared from their lives.
