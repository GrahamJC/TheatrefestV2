Hiya & Higher

You are cordially invited to attend an hour of laugh out loud entertainment in a friendly atmosphere that guarantees to leave you happier, and more confident and open minded than you were when you went in! Your hostess for the event will be Mrs Barbara Nice – mum of five, bargain hunter and advocate of a divine but seemingly dwindling human quality: friendliness!

In a world where an increasing section of society conducts a faceless social life via the internet and where we convince ourselves that we are “far too busy” for a chat in the supermarket, Mrs Nice reminds us that the simpler pleasures are often the most rewarding in life – saying “Hiya!” to an old friend or neighbour, rather than dodging around the aisles to avoid them, provides opportunities for laughter, happiness and perhaps even the chance to learn something new …

For warm fuzzies and belly laughs aplenty, you must see Hiya and Higher – it will change your life!
