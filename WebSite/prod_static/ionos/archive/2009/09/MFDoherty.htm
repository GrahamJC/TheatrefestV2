Fragments of Love

Mary-Frances Doherty


An exciting new performance that takes you on a journey of love, lust and jealousy.

During the course of the action the audience is offered an insight into a young woman’s hopes, dreams and aspirations of love juxtaposed with the harsh reality that a “bag of potatoes” will always be “just a bag of potatoes”.
