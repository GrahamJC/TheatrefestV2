When can I buy tickets?

You can buy tickets online or at the central box office until 30 minutes before the performance starts. After that time you can only buy tickets at the venue.

How can I pay for tickets?
Tickets bought online or at a venue must be paid for using a credit card. Tickets bought at the central box office can be paid for using cash or a credit card.
Who qualifies for concession tickets?
16 or under, students, over 60, unemployed or disabled.
What are eFringers and how do they work?
An eFringer Voucher can be used to buy tickets for 6 different performances at a discounted price.
  • eFringer Vouchers can only be bought online.
  • Vouchers can only be used to purchase tickets online.
  • Each eFringer Voucher can only be used to buy one ticket per performance.
  • Select Tickets and Fringers in "my account" to manage your eFringer Vouchers.
How will I get my tickets once I have bought them?
We do not use paper tickets. If you purchase tickets online (including use of eFringers) or at the central box office they will be validated at the venue by the e-mail address used when purchasing the tickets. A confirmation e-mail will also be sent for all tickets purchased online but you will not be asked to show this at the venue.
Can I cancel tickets?
Tickets bought using a credit card cannot be cancelled.
Tickets bought using an eFringer Voucher can be cancelled until 30 minutes before the performance begins. Select Tickets and Fringers in "my account" to manage your tickets.
