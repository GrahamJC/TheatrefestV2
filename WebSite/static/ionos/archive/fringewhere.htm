where did the fringe take place?

In Barnstaple.

On the Roundswell Industrial Estate (near Sainsbury)

In a specially constructed studio theatre space within The Factory - North Devon College's new music performance facility.

Part of the flavour of the Fringe is its transient, make-shift feel and this venue satisfied that whilst providing excellent facilities for performers and audiences alike.

In the foyer outside the performance area, musicians entertained audiences in between performances
