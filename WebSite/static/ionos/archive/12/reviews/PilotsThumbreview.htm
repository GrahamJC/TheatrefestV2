Cover Story

Pilot's Thumb Theatre


Cover Story begins with the tinkling of a piano but soon explodes into a gritty drama.  A pair of neighbours - Paul and Louise - arrive home from an night out. The first is an English teacher, a gentleman dressed in suitable attire, the other a lively, bubbly nurse in her thirtieth year. There is an awkward relationship between the two and this is amplified when the teacher is charged with a terrible crime. With brilliant acting from the cast, watch Paul as he frantically tries to explain his actions to an interviewer and twists the words swirling around his head, bringing the celebrations to an abrupt end.
