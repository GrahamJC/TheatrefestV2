Trouble and Strife

Sue Smith, Jane Castree and Jules Laville


Trouble and Strife opens with a cacophonous stream-of-consciousness soundtrack and accompanying dance that thrills and unsettles the audience in equal measure with an incredible display of harmony and athleticism. Next up is an enthralling and funny performance that takes the idea of the one man (or, more appropriately, the one woman) band and gives it and contemporary twist. The really special performance, however, is saved for last; in a dance that narrates the changing relationship between mother and daughter over time to a beautifully performed solo musical composition by singer/songwriter Rosa Rebecka. A challenging and thought provoking production that will leave viewers awe-struck and inspired.
