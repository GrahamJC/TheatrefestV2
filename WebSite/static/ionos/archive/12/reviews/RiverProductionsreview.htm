Revlon Scarlet Rescues the Crown Jewels

River Productions & Skylight Theatre


Skylight theatre bring you Revlon Scarlet – a hilarious swashbuckling trip back in time; solve clues, cheer on the goodies and indulge your inner pirate in this spectacular performance that all the family will enjoy. Join handsome time travelling pirate, Revlon Scarlet, his best friend George the penguin (with an Australian accent), his twin sister Sabre and Allison her chocolate eating pet ‘Vampasaur’ as they go on an exciting adventure across time and space in their time travelling bus to find treasure and save the queen’s all important crown jewels. All the while they must dodge their sworn enemy, the most mean, villainous and tyrannical captain to ever traverse the universe: Captain Bligh!
