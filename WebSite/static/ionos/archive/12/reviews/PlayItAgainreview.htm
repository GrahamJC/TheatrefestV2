Going Viral

Play It Again Theatre Company


Going Viral is a theatre piece that focuses on the perils that internet chat rooms and Facebook can bring into the lives of the careless or unwary user. Based on true stories, Going Viral centres on the lives of 3 people. Two experienced users of Facebook; aspiring models CJ  and Bella Jones “the hottest girl in school”, to one ‘internet newbie’, CJ’s Grandmother and how the lives of these 3 unwary people are changed for the worse by the more unscrupulous and cruel internet users. Going Viral is both dramatic and thought provoking,; an on topic drama that will be remembered by its audience due to it’s current theme and skilful acting.
