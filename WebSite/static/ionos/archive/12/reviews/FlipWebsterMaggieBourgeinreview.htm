Women of an Uncertain Age

Flip Webster & Maggie Bourgein

In terms of laughs-per-minute, Women of an Uncertain Age offer fantastic value for money. The show follows a ‘sketch show’ formula with Flip Webster and Maggie Bourgein undertaking a wide range of humorous roles. The ladies succeed in trying to be relatable to the audience through a lively mix of monologues, mimes, dances and songs including the very catchy G-R-A-double N-Y! Topics of on-stage discussion include grandmothers reliving their youth, the struggles of a single mother with four children and the ever present temptation of a bottle of Pinot Grigio. Dynamic, entertaining and borderline inappropriate, Women of an Uncertain Age will have you gasping for breath and demanding more.
