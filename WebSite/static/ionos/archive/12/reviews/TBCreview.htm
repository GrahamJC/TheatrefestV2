Absolute Improv!

To Be Continued...


The cosy and intimate setting of Boston Tea Party is perfect for a show like this. Absolute Improv is led by a trio of lively and enthusiastic people who find inspiration from the most outlandish things. Audience interaction and participation is the backbone of this show, so be prepared to say the first thing that pops into your head when you’re needed. Few productions make the same polished transition from act to act as this company does. A mix of rap contests, human puppeteering, interviews and an attempt to glamorize the Olympics will have you giggling and laughing throughout and will leave a smile on your face for many hours after.
