Luna

Pretty Good Girl Dance Theatre


Based on the award winning novel by Julie Anne Peters Luna tells the story of Liam, a repressed and introverted teen with one ambition that has consumed his life: to be accepted for who he is, under all the pretence and expectations. To be just like everyone else – as the woman he is on the inside. Superbly expressed through the medium of dance and emotionally narrated by his sister, Luna is a gripping, inspiring and moving drama about Liam’s struggle to be the woman he has always wanted to be, no matter how others react to his transformation and what society, his friends and his family want him to be.
