Fractal

Fractal


What’s better than three musicians? Three musicians and three dancers, obviously. The idea is quite simple; the musicians jam a bit, the dancers improvise to match the music, then the musicians feed off the dancers, and repeat; the result a “double-helix” of spontaneous artistic expression. When, in a bit of audience interaction, it was decided the next performance would be themed around a ‘Blue catastrophe’, I half wondered if we were in for a full medley from the former boy band. Rather, Fractal delivered a performance of brilliant quality and one you will be drawn to again should they return to the Fringe Theatrefest!! For a full return on the ticket price you should really bring a particularly un-inhibited friend, as there is an opportunity for audience members to get involved and do some dancing of their own; and the performers don’t mind if you film the whole thing.
