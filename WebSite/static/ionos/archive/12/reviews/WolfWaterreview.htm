a mid summer nights dream

Wolf + Water arts company/many directions theatre


This is a particularly moving piece of theatre performed with amazing enthusiasm by a cast comprising of adults with learning disabilities. The performance is interspersed with film clips depicting the events of the First World War, and has a backing track of incredibly evocative music. The audience are treated to some very comical moments, particularly when we are informed that Puck has ‘really messed up this time’.  The star of the show has to be the quick change artist in the form of Bottom, who, with a little assistance and a number of different masks, transforms into a multitude of different animals. I challenge you not to be moved by the exceptionally touching finale. Everyone involved in this production deserves a huge pat on the back.
