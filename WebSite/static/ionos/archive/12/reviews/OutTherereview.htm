Check In

Out There Theatre


See this show to watch a fast paced funny commentary, on almost every aspect of facebook, with a contemporary soundtrack. I found this performance very enjoyable, a great one for the locals, however the wider Theatre community will recognise and appreciate the comedy and the vernacular, even if they do not recognise the names and places. I couldn't look at facebook in quite the same way when I got home. In fact I enjoyed this so much, I'm going to go see it again if I can! "Like!" Out There Theatre truly took another step forwards, onwards..... and upwards, but hey, what did you expect, they are the three Giraffes!
Bryony Chave-Cox

 

Bath based performance company Out There Theatre returns to Fringe Theatrefest for their third year with this energetic cast. Laughter throughout, the audience find themselves associating with one (maybe all!) of the likable characters. Check In emphasizes all that we ‘like’ about social networking - and all that makes us cringe. On topic, professional theatre that includes fashion, cocktails, dating, cross-dressing, Facebook and the monotony of the everyday cuppa. A must see for the weekend, but do watch out for flying cocktail glasses (wink).
