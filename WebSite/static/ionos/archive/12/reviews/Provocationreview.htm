Bursary Boy

Provocation Theatre Company


Peter is on the cusp of becoming a Catholic priest. He is visiting his former mentor for a quick chat and despite their large age gap they are glad to see each other after all these years, particularly as Peter has reached this achievement. Nevertheless, it soon becomes apparent that their meeting won’t go so smoothly as the discussions continue. Simple small talk soon turns into a fiery argument! Each day reveals dark secrets that have remained untold for years. The audience will enjoy the humour in the play’s dialogue, but the desire for forgiveness and happiness is all that the duo strive for. Would you make allowances for such sins?
