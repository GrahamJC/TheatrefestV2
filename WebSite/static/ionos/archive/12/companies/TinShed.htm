Dr Frankenstein's Travelling Freak Show

Tin Shed Theatre Co.

"What does one see, when looking into the eyes of a monster?"

Dr Frankenstein invites you to revel in the freakish delights of his ram-shackle travelling freak show.

Tin Shed Theatre Co. retell Mary Shelly's Frankenstein through the debauched and twisted eyes of one cynical raconteur and his faithful gaggle of gormless goons.

This piece covers two very "Victorian-esque" themes, exploring the issues and ideas provoked by "The Freak Show" whist examaning the human need for companionship.

Darkly comical and sickly twisted, Dr Frankenstein's Travelling Freak Show treads the thin line between hilarity and morality. This piece is not to be missed.
