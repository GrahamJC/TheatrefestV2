Loose Tongues

Inkling Productions

Written by
Peter Almond, Ant Brown, Susan Offler, Sarah Paviour, Gillian Sweet-Bartley

‘Did you hear? No? Let me tell you…’

Unzipped. Several characters, not necessarily in search of the truth, loosen their tongues for a tale of spin, lying and misinformation that has grave consequences.

Directed by June Trask
