The Brown Parachute

White Hippos Productions

Over the years White Hippos Productions have gained a reputation for creating productions that mix fantasy and reality.

Little did they know that their efforts were miniscule compared to the fantasy and reality-bending antics of the financial industry.

Could it really be true that all over the world the biggest banks and building societies were buying and trading with money that didn't exist?

Sadly we all know the answer to that question, but several things still remain a mystery:

               Where did it all begin?

                           Who is to blame?   

                                                                                 Why has nobody been held accountable?


To find out the answer to these and many more simply ask yourself, what is The Brown Parachute? There are no tricky forms to fill in and the service is very friendly.

Join the White Hippos for a comic and irreverent investigation into the biggest fraud ever perpetrated against the human race.

Be honest, you have always really disliked your bank manager, now find out why.
