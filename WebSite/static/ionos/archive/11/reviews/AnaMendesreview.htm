Ana Mendes


Are you a ‘people watcher’? Ana Mendes invites you to spy into her life and examine human behaviour in a new and beautiful manner.

Is Ana questioned by the Police? Society? Or herself? In a country where we are constantly followed by CCTV, and have a fascination with Reality Television, Mendes grips the audience from the start.

The playwright uses memories to answer the many questions, and covers every subject, including sex, drugs and dreams.

A unique performance allowing sociology to meet comedy in perfect fusion.
