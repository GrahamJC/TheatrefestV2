Seize the Moment Presents

Seize the Moment


Enter a fairytale, where magic comes alive for the audience and the Brave warrior Princess.The performance was written, choreographed and produced by the young performers themselves that makes this show unique, and very special.

It captures the imagination of innocent youth with a mix of fantasy, time travelling, ghost stories, a dusting of fairy tales and surrealism.

The delightful use of props made from bin liners and buckets, to paper bags that gives this show a breath of fresh air.

A magical performance that is funny, enthusiastic and bursting with energy.
