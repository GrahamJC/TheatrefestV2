The Songster's Tale

Pickpocket Productions


‘Pete Buffery’ gives a fabulous performance with a contemporary take on a tale of love and adventure. The intimate setting and subtle lighting with just ‘Pete’ and his guitar absorb you fully, bringing this magical one-man show alive. A film projection on the back wall accompanies him whilst he tells his own, original adaption of the story the ‘Knights Tale’ through his music and songs. Crazy visual surreal images give this timeless tale of love a quirky modern feel, along with a mix of soulful singing that will have you transfixed with watching this enjoyable, theatrical show.

Rachel Perham
