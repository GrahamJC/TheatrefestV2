studio@QT and gallery@QT are both in the Queen's Theatre - where the central box-office is also located.

studio@QT is a reconfiguration of the main auditorium that provides a generous studio theatre. gallery@QT is a reconfiguration of the gallery cafe - regularly used for music and spoken word events - as an intimate studio theatre.

The Queen's Theatre box-office is also the meeting point for Venue X which is a short journey away.

The Baptist Hall is at the top end of Boutport Street, next to the Baptist Church. This fine historical building provides a large enough space to accommodate the dance events in Fringe TheatreFest plus the large-cast extravaganzas.

Boston Tea Party will be a venue for productions on Thursday and Saturday evening and on Sunday afternoon. BTP will also be hosting a series of lunchtime 'taster' previews of Fringe TheatreFest productions and is a great place for good coffee. The 'taster' sessions will start at 1.00pm and last for an hour and will be happening from Wed 22nd through to Sat 25th.
