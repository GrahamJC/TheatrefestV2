booking information




tickets are on sale from June 1st

Tickets for each event cost £5/£3 concessions (16 or under, students, over 60, unemployed, disabled).

Tickets can be pre-booked at the Queen’s Theatre, Barnstaple and Landmark Theatre, Ilfracombe - (01271) 32 42 42.

Tickets can also be purchased on the day of performance, at the appropriate venue, one hour before the performance starts.


Frequent Fringer tickets cost £15 and allow entry to 6 performances of your choice (provided seats are available). These can be pre-purchased from the Queen’s Theatre or Landmark Theatre or from any of the venues.


Fringe Buttons

Every audience member aged 13+ needs a Fringe Button to enter a venue.

These are a one-off purchase of £1 from any of the venues.

Money from the Fringe Buttons goes towards the infrastructure costs of fringe theatrefest whereas all income from ticket sales goes directly to the performers.
