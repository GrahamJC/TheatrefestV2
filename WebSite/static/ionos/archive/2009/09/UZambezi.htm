U'Zambezi Performing Arts


U'Zambezi Arts are the UK based Southern African song, dance, drummers and related arts troupe.

The group draws its membership from arts consultants, arts managers, arts education specialists and arts enthusiasts who all came together for the love of their Southern African traditions and heritages.

What brings them together is their love for what they do and are so very proud to share. Some members' CVs and profiles include performing for such dignitaries as Nelson Mandela and Queen Elizabeth II amongst others.
