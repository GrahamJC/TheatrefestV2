Lady In Bed

This is a high energy show that ingeniously blends laugh out loud comedy with elements of true poignancy. Alison Goldie presents us with a funny and intimate autobiographical account of one woman’s quest to find Mr Right (although occasionally she only manages to meet Mr Right Now!)

Through her revelations to her naïve teenage self, Goldie invites us to follow her epic journey of love and lust – a journey which spans over thirty years and takes her across the seas to America, Greece and Spain and into the arms of an exotic stranger or two! We meet a multitude of characters along the way including an assortment of lovers, her mother, her straight talking best friend and even a couple of birds in New York!
