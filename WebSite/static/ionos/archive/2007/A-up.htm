Beach Impediment Episode 3… Christmas

All summer long Dave works and lives in a hut on the beach selling pasties and tea to holiday-makers. Through the winter, when all the tourists have gone home, he is joined by his surfer friend Tom. Together they keep the beach clean, contemplate the meaning of life and go on a series of mind-bending adventures.

Beach Impediment is a radio show owing much to The Goons and The Hitch Hikers Guide To The Galaxy, in its anarchic mix of sophisticated existential humour and downright bad jokes.

Episode Three will be recorded in front of a live audience as part of the Festival Fringe and you are invited to take part, (laughing, we hope, and shouting things like, “Its behind you!” and “Custard!”)

Episode 1... Dunes and Episode 2... Halloween will be available on podcast, as soon as we find the on button.
