suggested accommodation

www.staynorthdevon.co.uk
The official Tourist Information Centre website with a comprehensive list of what's on offer.

Barn Cottage
Middle Hearson, Swimbridge, Barnstaple, Devon, EX32 0QH
Strongly recommended, not least because the proprietor is a keen Fringe supporter. The accommodation is a few miles out of Barnstaple so your own transport is a must but it is particularly lovely and gives a great flavour of what North Devon has to offer.

The Inn on the Square
The Square, Barnstaple, North Devon EX32 8LS
One of the Fringe venues, slap bang in the centre of town. If you want to sleep, eat and breathe the Fringe then this is for you.

The Wrey Arms
A large pub near The Factory - one of the Fringe venues.

Cresta Guest House
Probably the best value and located near The Factory.

Little Orchard
Braunton Road, Barnstaple, EX31 1GA. Tel: 01271 371714
One of the cheapest Bed & Breakfast possibilities

Mount Sandford
Landkey Road, Newport, Barnstaple, EX32 0HL. Tel: 01271 342354
A little further out - easier with a car

Rosehill
Deer Park Road, Barnstaple, EX32 0PD. Tel: 01271 329931
Not far from the centre of town

Kingston House
Convenient for the centre

Rowden Barton
Convenient for The Factory

Lower Yelland Farm
Would need transport or rely on taxis (about 4 miles from Barnstaple)

The Tannery
Would need transport or rely on bus/taxis (about 4 miles from Barnstaple)

Sunnymeade
About 20 minutes walk from The Factory

Travelodge
Five minutes walk from The Factory

Premier Inn
Prices are per room, so sharing halves the price. Walkable from the centre of town
