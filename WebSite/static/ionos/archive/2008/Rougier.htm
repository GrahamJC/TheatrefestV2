Robinson Crusoe - All Washed Up

a Mime Musical

Fergus Rougier's theatrical retelling of this timeless classic about a shipwrecked guy, Man-Friday, cannibals and a parrot called Polly. Told through an innovative mix of physical storytelling, mime and song by an international performer and his accordion.

Imaginative, beautiful, very funny and with great tunes, this is a truly unique one-man show.

Touring the UK, USA, Canada and Japan during summer 2008.
