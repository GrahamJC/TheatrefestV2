Midsummer Margaret

Pre-teenage relationships erupt close to the edge.

Forches Finest - a group of 10 and 11 year olds from Forches Cross Primary School - have taken ideas from "A Midsummer Night's Dream" and put them in a modern day local setting. The play is a movement and dance-based drama, incorporating computer imagery.
