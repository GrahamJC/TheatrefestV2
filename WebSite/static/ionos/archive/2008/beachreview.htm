Beach Impediment - episode 2

Beach Impediment is an enjoyable bit of silliness - a "radio play" live on stage, performed script in hand with live sound effects which allow for some good visual gags. The five strong company peform the Halloween themed adventure with gusto, clearly enjoying the groan inducing puns and absurd storyline. Tom Nordon's homely tea addicted witch (one of three snap-playing witches encountered on a ghost-hunting expedition) was a personal highlight.

Frederica Notley
