A Room Full of Elephants

A hotel bedroom: place of rendez-vous, recreation or refuge for a series of guests with more or less – or not at all – licit purposes: each story shaded and shaped by the agenda of its own particular elephant.  Intelligent, believable, bitter sweet: more than a dozen parts beautifully played by this talented cast of just four.

Ginny Hobart (official Fringe reviewer)
