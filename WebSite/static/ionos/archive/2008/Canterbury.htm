The Canterbury Tales

Fresh from a barnstorming tour of North Devon villages, Pilton-based drama group Lord Basil's Reformers present a new, vigorous telling of several of Chaucer's classic stories. Share Chaucer's take on life, warts and all, as you watch the travellers' stories unfold. Treachery, lust and avarice are writ large in this testament to the human condition.
