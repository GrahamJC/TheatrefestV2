Dodgy Keeper

Dodgy Keeper, from Stretch Mark Productions, is a one man show about a goal keeper and his various complex relationships. It mixes dark comic drama, high octane energy, poetry and stand-up. This goalie has a dark secret, it's going to cost him everything. He's even gambled the team's money on a dodgy horse. As for his family - his wife is seemingly having an affair and his nine-year-old daughter appears to know more about his life than he does...

The play is written and performed by Harry Mottram (who has played numerous times in goal) and directed by John Bailey of Axbridge Community Theatre.
