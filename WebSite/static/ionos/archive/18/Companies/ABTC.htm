“With magic seeds, water and light,
soon you will see a big delight.
Purple, green and rather round -
an Enormous turnip will grow up from the ground!”


Above Bounds Theatre Collective invite you to join them for a magical storytelling workshop, inspired by the well-loved tale The Enormous Turnip. With crafty puppet making and interactive storytelling, this is the perfect activity for all the family. Plus, during the workshop you will make your own peg puppet to take home!

Above Bounds Theatre Collective previously performed Travellers' Tales at Fringe TheatreFest in 2016, and their production of The Ugly Duckling & Other Tales appeared at The Plough Arts Centre in August 2017.
