The award-winning true story of the most famous comedy duo of all time. Witness Stan and Ollie's meteoric rise to fame, the decline of their movie careers and phoenix-like revival on the stage. Touring since 2013, be prepared to laugh your socks off before having your heart broken.

Hats Off to Laurel & Hardy has played all over the UK and in Los Angeles, Berlin and Prague. It is generally accepted as being the most accurate play ever written about the pair.
