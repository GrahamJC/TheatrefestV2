Suitable for children aged 5 and up, The Fisher-Knight's Tale is a pop-up adventure for intimate settings. Meet Laura, the Fisher-Knight! She is brave, she is bold, and she is armed with a fishing rod! Laura is on a perilous quest to save the River Kingdom from floods, and there are plenty of characters to help her along the way. But where have all the fish gone!? And who is cutting down all the trees?

Based in South Devon, Wild Toy Theatre is a fledgling company creating puppet theatre on an intimate scale. We tell original stories inspired by rural themes, including wildlife, folklore and local heritage.
