We live in chaotic times; problems and dilemmas confront us at every turn.

Throughout history the people of this planet have turned to its greatest thinkers to provide solutions to our problems.

None of them have ever decided to use improvisational comedy to provide them.

Until now.

Using nothing but his wits and improvisational skills, Sam Gibbs will save the World.

From the creators of The Idea, Beowulf,TALL and The Boy Who Fell in Love with the Stars.

Autojeu Theatre and White Hippos Productions, two Fringe Theatrefest favourites, unite to bring you a hilarious new show.

If you have a problem, if no one else can help, and if you can find him, maybe you can hire the Problem Solver... equity rates apply.
