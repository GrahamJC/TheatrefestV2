Greenwich Village comes to Barnstaple - pt2

Dave Clinch

music and song
all ages    20 mins at a time

  • various cafes
  • Saturday 11am - 1.30pm

A musical journey around some of the independent cafés of Barnstaple in the spirit of Greenwich Village in the early 1960s.

Local musicians wlll be performing 20 minute sets at each venue:

Barnstaple Museum Café, The Square
Tea by the Taw,  The Strand
Nibbles Sandwich Bar,  Butchers’ Row
Lemon Next to the Pie,  Holland Walk
Driftwood & The Carvery,  Gammon Walk

All Proceeds to Save Our Hospital Services Campaign Devon
