Moving On

Wild & Blue Projects

street-theatre, dance
all ages
60 mins

  • Barnstaple town centre
  • Saturday 12.30

  • bucket collection

  • Barnstaple town centre
  • Saturday noon

Devon based contemporary dancers and visiting Fringe performers lead groups of curious movers of all ages in a mesmerising, improvised dance .....noticing, remarking, tracing, following, exploring & experiencing the streets of Barnstaple. Gradually the groups find each other and mingle in a celebration of the life around them. Do you see what they see, hear what they hear, imagine what they imagine? Follow and find out.
