Celebrity Love Hero

Hold onto you seats for this fast paced show! Expect the unexpected, as Philip Douch has you laughing from the moment you sit down. With some audience participation you can laugh with your friends as celebrities come alive, the stage becomes transformed, and you are taken on a very special journey. All topics of Love and Lust are covered, in lively and musical splendor. An Individual man, talking about the individuality of names, for a unique and excellent show, with much, much hilarity.

Annabelle Mantziou The Fringe Secrets
