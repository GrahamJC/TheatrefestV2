Small Space

This play is set within Dan and Jane's own kitchen, where they reveal to us that they are married and have sex but then inform us that beyond this, everything they to tell us will be a lie.

They regress to childhood memories, not always as they might seem, and reminisce about Dan's proposal to Jane in New York City, neither recalling the same sequence of events.

This drama, set in such personal surroundings, is based upon intimacy. It is poignant, moving and performed to perfection by Dan Milne and Jane Nash. Every single lie is utterly believable.

Please make every effort to see this fabulous and compelling drama. Seats are limited due to the nature of the location.

Sheila Wilson The Fringe Secrets
