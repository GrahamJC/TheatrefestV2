The Greenish Room

Actress of the moment, young sports personality of the year and bad boy comedian meet in the green room of a Saturday night talk show.

Worried about the questions they will be asked by the talk show host, they agree to stick to their principles and not answer the usual superficial questions.

As the audience, we are witness to a progression over time in which we see the characters develop, as their principles and personalities adapt to the realities and harsh blows that life has dealt them.

A challenging piece of comedy drama with some very funny lines - "Comedy is a nasty business; it's all whisky and insults".

The cast coped particularly well with a script delivered only 5 days ago.

Sheila Wilson The Fringe Secrets
