The Show Must Go On

Be prepared for anything with this magical extravaganza of storytelling, puppetry, music and comedy. The gravelly voiced puppet Miranda, (reminiscent of Marlene Dietrich) steals the show and audience participation is definitely expected. This production is funny from the outset, expect the unexpected and you won't be disappointed.

The 4 members (and Miranda) of Unpacked make an hour pass in a matter of minutes so, sit back and enjoy a truly different type of musical comedy from this immensely funny and talented cast.

If you only see one puppet show this year make absolutely sure it's this one!

Sheila Wilson The Fringe Secrets
