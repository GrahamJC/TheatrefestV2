Raggle-Taggle

Prepare for a tale to unfold before you, with music, media, and troubadours! Gripping your imagination from the beginning, Paul Southcott's voice takes you to a land where magical Romany sounds come alive. The many instruments skilfully played by Peter Stone will surprise and delight audiences as they long to stand up and dance! With Gypsy fables coming alive, and Folk music from the very start, it is impossible not to enjoy every second of Raggle Taggle, young or old.

Annabelle Mantziou The Fringe Secrets
