(Untitled)

This production is in its infancy and as a work in progress suggestions and feedback are welcomed by the company. Performed by six young members of the Openbatch Theatre Company the story takes place within the walls of an imaginary household.

Linked together by a series of sketches, some mimed and others with dialogue, we go through a rollercoaster of different emotions. Although mainly aimed at making us laugh there are one or two poignant moments. The production challenges the confines of the home and the choices made within those confines.

The finale - "In My House" - is hilariously funny. A clever, funny and thought provoking piece performed by a very talented young cast.

Sheila Wilson The Fringe Secrets
