Sky-Larking (Other People's Stories)

His Jeweled Floor

A one-man show featuring drama, comedy, dance, movement theatre, poetry, a live painting and a hopeful Malaysian accountant who, due to redundancy, is looking for his purpose and identity. Going out into the world with the aid of his friends: Joe the painter; Seb the poet; Rufus the adventurer and Maya his heroine, Dolah seeks what he thinks he has lost.
