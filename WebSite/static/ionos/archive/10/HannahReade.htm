Night-Songs

Hannah Reade

Hannah invites you to write a song with her based on a dream that you've had.

Join her in a quiet area of The Inn on the Square for a short meeting to write and sing together.

All participants will receive a CD of the gathered Night-Songs.

 

 

one-on-one music-making
age 16+ or accompanied by an adult
30 minute sessions
