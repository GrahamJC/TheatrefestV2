who's putting this fringe together?

multi story theatre company (aka Gill Nathanson and Bill Buffery) are leading the project with a huge amount of support from North Devon Festival and the North Devon Theatres Trust. Support from North Devon College who made their venue, The Factory, available, was a crucial factor in the success of the 07 Fringe TheatreFest.

why?

Because multi story were inspired by their experiences on the Canadian Fringe Theatre circuit 8 years ago and they want North Devon to have some of the fun.

isn't it a bit much for two people to administrate, given that they're 'on the road' most of the time?

We want to get it started - grow it carefully for a year or two. Hopefully it will quickly become too large for us to manage and we'll have to look at how to sustain a larger version in future years.


But for now...
