Broken Angels

Thrice Three Muses

theatre, discussion, forum
age 14+ Contains true life accounts of anorexia nervosa.
Sufferers and/or vulnerable adults may wish to bring a friend.
45 mins + 15 mins discussion

  • The Baptist Hall
  • Thursday 7.30pm
  • Friday 7.15pm

“How’s it going?”
You will ask.
I’ll reply, “It’s good,
And you?”
I’ve lied so long, the truth seems wrong.
A hidden thought – unless you knew…

A woman (or man) is alone in her room. She's sent all her friends away, except one. Her ‘lump’. An honest, vivid portrait of mental illness.
