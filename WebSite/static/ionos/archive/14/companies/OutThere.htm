Smidge

Out There Theatre

drama
age 12+
40 mins

  • gallery@QT
  • Thursday 5pm
  • Saturday 3pm
  • Sunday 6.45pm

A beautifully bittersweet tale of friendship and creativity; Smidge will take you back to a world you've not quite forgotten. A world of childish imagination and wonder. In case you don't know, Smidge is really purple and has feet that are springs and legs that are not with a big fluffy belly and really curly ears. Usually grown-ups can't see Smidge, but hopefully you'll be able to.

A marvel of movement, shadow, film, sound art and imagination, these are the mémoirs d'un ami imaginaire...
