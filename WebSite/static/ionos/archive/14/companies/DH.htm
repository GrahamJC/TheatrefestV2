Poetry Ping Pong

Daniel Haynes and Robert Garnham

spoken word
age 14+
55 mins

  • Barnstaple Library
  • Friday 5pm & 6.30pm
  • Room At the Top
  • Saturday 2pm & 5pm
  • Sunday 1pm & 4pm

It's 50000 AD and a new bloodsport rules... Legends of poetry through the ages are resurrected to battle in the deadly game they call Poetry Ping Pong. Only two remain...

Daniel and Robert present a fast paced hour of original and entertaining comic poetry.

Daniel Haynes has won two poetry slams and is the current bard of Exeter.

Robert Garnham is a slam winner, host of Poetry Island and has performed nationally and internationally with renowned acts such as Nathan Filer, Rob Auton and John Hegley.


“Robert is the duck-billed platypus of British poetry” - Jackie Juno

“Daniel is utterly unique, insanely clever and hilariously surreal” - Gina Sherman, Apples and Snakes.
