Meet the Real Maggie Thatcher

Gerundagula Productions

contemporary drama
age 15+
45 mins

  • The Baptist Hall
  • Friday 6pm
  • Saturday 3.45pm
  • Sunday 5pm

Coinciding with the 30th anniversary of the Miner’s Strike a highly original concept in modern drama by Liverpool's radical touring company, Meet the Real Maggie Thatcher provides an engaging insight into one of the most notorious political leaders of our era. The performance contains no strong language… only the truth.

Barnstaple's Fringe TheatreFest 2014 will be both the UK premiere performance and the start of a national tour. Due to the nature of the piece there will be a short after show question and answer session with the cast and writer at all performances. Award winning writer Mike Francis Carvalho is most recently renowned for 80’s themed drama Vienna and the controversial Edinburgh Fringe 2011 production “Breathe in”. Meet the Real Maggie Thatcher is directed by the legendary ‘illy’ formerly of BBC, Granada and Mersey Television.
