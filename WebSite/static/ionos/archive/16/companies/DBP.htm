Drive By is a one to one performance set in a car. There you will meet Sophie, and together you will navigate your way through the many ups and downs of life. The road may be a little bumpy at times, but don't worry, you'll be safe.

The questions is - When your at a crossroads, do you break or accelerate? Which one will you choose? Unique to each individual. This piece is witty, heartwarming, genuine and has a charming quality.

"The only thing I would change is to carry on along the journey for longer, I wanted more!" Laura Hall, Community Outreach Manager at The Everyman and Playhouse, Liverpool.
