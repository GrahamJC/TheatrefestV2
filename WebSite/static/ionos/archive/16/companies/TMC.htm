You Have Mail

The Monday Collective

interactive participation scenario
age 10+
45 mins

  • meet at Queen's Theatre
  • Thursday 8.15pm
  • Friday 6.30pm & 8.00pm
  • Saturday 6.30pm & 8.00pm

A message has arrived from ... somewhere, which must be understood and shared. Others may be depending on it. The audience are the messengers and we're tied up in something bigger than all of us. Don't shoot the messengers ... They're only playing.

This performance piece is an interactive game for a maximum of eight people at each performance. A mysterious scenario gradually unfolds involving the audience in problem solving and code breaking. The performers will lead the audience through various tasks using means of communication that may be distant, remote, old fashioned and digital, in order to achieve a goal. Failure is not a possibility. Success is imperative.
