Under African Skies

Gary Cordingley

stroytelling
age 8- 98  
60 mins

  • Room At The Top
  • Thursday 5pm
  • Friday 5.30pm
  • Saturday 1pm & 5pm
  • Sunday 3.30pm

Under African Skies is a single handed storytelling performance with live music, singing, drumming and audience participation aimed at family audiences.

The story follows the adventures of Mamadou, a young boy who runs away from his village, and is a mixture of traditional African stories under an original narrative, told by professional storyteller, Gary Cordingley.
