The Sentence

Lytton Productions

drama
age 10+
60 mins

  • St Anne's Arts Centre
  • Friday 7pm
  • Saturday 5pm
  • Sunday 4pm

The Sentence is the story of John Scrivener who in the 16th century was sentenced to death by burning. His crime was to translate the Bible into English. His daughter Joan was sentenced to light the fire. Both are the victims of illogical and unreasonable rules which have an angry redolence for anyone who is confronted by an insane enforcement of law which is outdated, outmoded and cruel.

This powerful two actor play explores the themes of language and justice looking at the difficulties facing people who dare to challenge authority.
