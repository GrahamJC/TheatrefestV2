Brickhead

age 12+
60 mins

  • gallery@QT

  • Saturday 8.30pm
  • Sunday 1pm

A clown show for adults!

Absurd Lecoq-trained clown Brickhead takes you on a journey to worlds both weird and profound. An hour of bonkers, nonsensical fun for those who like their humour weird and wacky!

A Lecoq-based training at LISPA and a member of acclaimed theatre company Intuitive Creatures, Brickhead ventures out on his own with a solo clown show exploring things… like… yeah.

Fresh from successful runs at the 2015 Brighton, Prague and Edinburgh Fringe Festivals, Brickhead's show is an absurd, intimate and heartfelt experience not to be missed.
