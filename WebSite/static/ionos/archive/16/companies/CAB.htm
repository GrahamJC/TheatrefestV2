Death and the Data Processor

The Cock and Bulls

comedy-drama
age 14+ strong language
60 mins

  • St Anne's Arts Centre
  • Thursday 7pm
  • Friday 4pm
  • Saturday 2.15pm

 

After ‘accidentally’ killing his boss, Ian must find himself a safe haven, and quick. Finding what seems like the village of his dreams, Ian embarks on a trek into the criminal underworld as you have never seen it before…

Expect laughs, thrills, and some extreme puppet violence.
