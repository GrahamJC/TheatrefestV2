Cinco Coisas

Bianca Bertalot


Who'd have thought that so much wit, humour and energy could come from just one woman?

Bianca Bertalot presents a hilarious show that merges dance, play, clowning and improvisation. I was laughing throughout, as her perfect use of timing and wit creates an unstoppable force of entertainment.

The energy levels on stage were incredible, particularly impressive as it was just one performer. There were two opposing characters which were very well acted, their mannerisms and reactions adding to the humour. Interactions with the audience also added to the fun and made us feel really involved.

But among the laughs, there are poignant and thought provoking moments. The play is focused on the five basic developmental needs of a child: Place, Nurturing, Support, Protection and Limitation. These are well presented, leading the audience to consider their own childhood.

Cinco Coisas is certainly a show packed full of fun, with a range of different performance styles and an abundance of energy. It is definitely one that I would recommend.
Seraphina Allard – Bridge
