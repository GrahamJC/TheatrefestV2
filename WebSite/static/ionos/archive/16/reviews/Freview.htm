My First Ten Sexual Failures and Other Stories About Growing Up

Fragility


A proficient raconteur, providing pathos and pleasure to an open minded adult audience.

A one man show starring a puny unfortunate young fellow who found it necessary to strip naked with the aid of a small teddy bear to prove this point. Despite his limited physique his agility and enthusiasm never faltered whether he was climbing over the audience or performing gymnastics with a chair whilst in a state of undress. The relevance of these antics to his tale of woe, escapes me but, it was entertaining and I wouldn’t have missed it for the world.

Funny, sad, annoying, poignant recollection of his failed attempts to find romance and a long term relationship both at home and away confirmed his status as “Loser”. Even his little ditty with his ukulele for company didn’t convince the audience of his prowess with the opposite sex or instil much faith in his future prospects with “the ladies”.

An energetic funny and talented performance.

Do not miss this one!
Chris Brown, The Daily Fringe
