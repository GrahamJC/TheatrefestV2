9ft²

A Hammier Basis


What does life feel like in a space with an area 3 feet by 3 feet? We can all identify with the clever portrayals by this company of how people and space react together in our modern world, which is full of loneliness, fear and just plain silliness. Edgy, intense, obscure and yet funny, this is great stuff. The ideas were very inventive, cleverly and confidently performed in a series of monologues and scenes by three very talented actors.

The actors explore the madness of how we share our space and our relationships with others within that often claustrophobic space and the performance is indeed at times dark and irreverent, yet light and playful and thought provoking – it's certainly taken my idea of that very modern phenomenon called 'de-cluttering' to a whole new level!

Although I have to admit to finding myself wondering if I was 'on the right page' at times, this comment from the audience describes the performance most succinctly…

“Hilarious, introspective, mad-magic!”
Lesley Gaish, The Daily Fringe
