High Vis

Monkeydog Productions

Robert Cohen's one-man tragicomedy about Quint, a traffic warden under siege. As he shelters reluctantly from an airgun-wielding stalker, Quint will teach you all about traffic management... and much more besides.


***** “Robert Cohen’s ability to balance the comedic with the tragic is superb… more High Vis please and more Quint!”
The Latest 7 magazine

**** “This is the sort of skill that Peter Sellers had, and so did Leonard Rossiter… Each time I see Robert Cohen perform, within moments I've entirely forgotten it is Robert Cohen. That's good isn't it? Very, very good.”
Fringe Review

**** “High Vis is in the spirit of Coronation Street or the great British Kitchen Sink Drama in that it scratches the surface of the salt-of-the-earth Northerner in ways that are both highly comic and bleakly tragic.”
