Beowulf

Autojeu Theatre


For fringe performances I like to keep an open mind and dive in to each show without too much preparation as a viewer - I like surprises! Although I have encountered Beowulf on the page before I had no idea what to expect from this show. I LOVED it! Sam Gibbs (Autojeu) is a charismatic performer. Single handedly he strode, swam and marched through the epic, dragon slaying tale delighting the audience with his Peter Kaye-esque funny faces and captivating mime. One man band Pete Buffery enhanced the atmosphere of the magical tale with his wonderful guitar and voice and musical sound effects.

Amanda McCormack, Arts & Entertainment
