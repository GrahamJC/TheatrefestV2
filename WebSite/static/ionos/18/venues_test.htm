The Baptist Hall

Here is where your company logo and message will be if you sponsor the programme in The Baptist Hall. Find out more.

The Baptist Hall is at the top end of Boutport Street, next to the Baptist Church. This fine, historic building provides a space large enough to house a pop-up 60 seat studio theatre, big enough to accomodate dance and some of the larger-cast extravaganzas.

The Guildhall

Here is where your company logo and message will be if you sponsor the programme in The Guildhall. Find out more.

The Guildhall is one of Barnstaple's most picturesque historic building and has been made available to Fringe TheatreFest thanks to Barnstaple Town Council . This atmospheric venue boasts a large performance area and raked seating that holds 80.

St Anne's Arts Centre

St Anne's Arts & Community Centre provides an adaptable 50-seat setting for innovative work. The building itself is well worth a visit, steeped, as it is, in history. The Plough Arts Centre programme St Anne's and have made the venue available to us.

Castle Centre

Here is where your company logo and message will be if you sponsor the programme in the Castle Centre. Find out more.

Castle Centre provides a 60-seat end-on venue with a low raised stage. It is a well-focussed venue, suitable for small-scale studio productions. This is the first time that Fringe TheatreFest has used this venue which is hired to us by the Town Council.
shows



The Southgate

Here is where your company logo and message will be if you sponsor the programme in The Southgate. Find out more.

The Southgate is a fine eatery between High Street and the Square. The 30-seater first-floor venue is an ideal, focussed space for stand-up comedy and intimate drama. Audiences are welcome to take up drinks - alcoholic or teas and coffees - from the bar.
shows



The Golden Lion Tap

Here is where your company logo and message will be if you sponsor the programme in The Golden Lion Tap. Find out more.

The Golden Lion Tap is an old coaching inn that borders The Square. The management have generously made available the large back room that is well-known in the town as a music venue. This will be configured as a studio/cabaret space with room for 60.
shows



The Tent on the Square

Here is where your company logo and message will be if you sponsor the programme in The Tent on the Square. Find out more.

The Tent on the Square was a great success last year. It's a sturdy structure that belongs to The Common Players and is configured into an atmospheric 60-seat venue. It's the most immediately visible of all the venues and also acts as an information hub.
