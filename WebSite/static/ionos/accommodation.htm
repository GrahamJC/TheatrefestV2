suggested accommodation

www.staynorthdevon.co.uk
The official Tourist Information Centre website with a comprehensive list of what's on offer including hotels & B&B, self-catering and camping. Brightlycott Barton has proved to be a particularly popular campsite in the past.



If you're looking to make Fringe TheatreFest part of a longer stay in beautiful North Devon, check out the range of accommodation offered by myfavouriteholidaycottages.co.uk


Devon holidays - another possible one-stop solution with a variety of accommodation on offer.


The Golden Lion Tap is one of this year's venues, so you could stay on site. They have seven en-suite rooms available. Let them know you're with Fringe TheatreFest when discussing the price.


The Olive Branch
41 Boutport Street, Barnstaple,Devon EX31 1SE
A town centre pub located very close to the Fringe venues offering accommodation at a reasonable price.


The Poplars B&B
In Rumsam, a quiet residential area within easy walking distance of the centre of Barnstaple. Exceptional accommodation. Tel 01271 378773.


Yeo Dale Hotel
Pilton Bridge, Barnstaple, EX31 1PG. Tel 01271 379120.
Popular choice for performers and audience alike. 400 yards from the town centre.


Cresta Guest House
Good value but a little bit of a walk up Sticklepath Hill - three-quarters of a mile. 01271 374022


Lower Yelland Farm
Would need transport or rely on taxis (about 4 miles from Barnstaple)


Premier Inn
Prices are per room, so sharing halves the price. Walkable from the centre of town
